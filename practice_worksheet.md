## Comparison Operators - Help Box

| Operator | Meaning | Example |
|----------|---------|---------|
| `>` | greater than | `age > 12` |
| `<` | less than | `score < 50` |
| `>=` | greater than or equal | `points >= 100` |
| `<=` | less than or equal | `time <= 18` |
| `==` | equal to | `name == "Max"` |
| `!=` | not equal to | `color != "red"` |
